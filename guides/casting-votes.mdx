Casting a vote in VoteLab requires a connected wallet and payment of a small entry fee. Each vote is recorded on-chain and contributes to the community prize pool.

## Prerequisites

Before you can cast a vote, ensure:

- Your wallet is [connected](/guides/connecting-wallet) to VoteLab
- You're on the correct network (Anvil or Sepolia)
- You have at least **0.01 ETH** plus gas fees in your wallet
- The voting session is in the **VOTING** phase (workflow station 1)

<Warning>
You can only vote once per election session. Once you've cast your vote, you cannot change it until the next election begins.
</Warning>

## Entry Fee

Each vote requires a payment of **0.01 ETH**, which is added to the community prize pool. This entry fee:

- Prevents spam and Sybil attacks
- Creates a prize pool for the community
- Ensures participants have skin in the game

<Info>
The prize pool is visible on the dashboard and accumulates throughout the voting session.
</Info>

## How to Cast Your Vote

<Steps>
  <Step title="Wait for Voting Phase">
    Voting is only allowed during the **VOTING** phase. You'll see the proposal question and two voting cards displayed on the dashboard.
    
    If you see "Governance Standby," the voting hasn't started yet. Wait for the contract owner to start the voting session.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the Proposal">
    Read the proposal question carefully. For example:
    
    > "Which fruit provides the best energy for coding sessions?"
    
    Review both options and their current vote tallies before making your decision.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Choice">
    Click the "Cast Your Vote" button on the voting card for your preferred option:
    
    - **Option A** (optionId: 0) - Displayed on the left card
    - **Option B** (optionId: 1) - Displayed on the right card
    
    Each card shows:
    - Current vote count ("Live Support")
    - Percentage of total votes ("Network Share")
    - Visual progress bar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the Transaction">
    Your wallet will prompt you to approve the transaction. Review the details:
    
    - **Amount**: 0.01 ETH (entry fee)
    - **Gas fee**: Variable based on network congestion
    - **Total**: 0.01 ETH + gas
    
    Click "Confirm" in your wallet to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    The voting card will display a "Processing..." state with a loading animation. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on-chain.
    
    This typically takes:
    - **Anvil**: Nearly instant (local network)
    - **Sepolia**: 15-30 seconds (testnet)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Vote">
    Once confirmed, you'll see:
    
    - The button changes to "Vote Recorded" with a green checkmark
    - A green badge appears: "Recorded On-Chain"
    - The vote tally updates automatically
    - You can no longer vote again in this session
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Vote Function Implementation

Here's how the `castVote` function works under the hood:

```typescript
const castVote = (option: number) => {
    writeContract({
        address: ContractAddress,
        abi: ABI,
        functionName: "vote",
        args: [BigInt(option)],
        value: parseEther("0.01"),
    });
};
```

The function:
- Accepts an `option` parameter (0 or 1)
- Calls the `vote` function on the smart contract
- Sends 0.01 ETH as the entry fee using `parseEther("0.01")`
- Records your vote on-chain with your wallet address

<Note>
The application automatically refetches all data after a successful transaction, including vote counts, prize pool, and your voting status.
</Note>

## Voting States

The voting card adapts based on the current state:

### Active Voting
- **Button**: White background, "Cast Your Vote" text
- **Behavior**: Clickable and ready to accept votes
- **Condition**: Voting is open AND you haven't voted

### Processing
- **Button**: Shows loading spinner and "Processing..." text
- **Behavior**: Disabled while transaction confirms
- **Condition**: Transaction submitted, waiting for confirmation

### Already Voted
- **Button**: Green background, "Vote Recorded" text with checkmark
- **Behavior**: Disabled, cannot vote again
- **Condition**: You've already voted in this election

### Voting Closed
- **Button**: Gray background, disabled state
- **Behavior**: Clicking does nothing
- **Condition**: Voting session is not in VOTING phase

## After Voting

Once you've cast your vote:

1. Your vote is permanently recorded on-chain
2. Your 0.01 ETH is added to the prize pool
3. You can still view live results as others vote
4. You'll see the "Recorded On-Chain" badge on the dashboard
5. You cannot vote again until the next election session

<Info>
The contract tracks which election ID you voted in. When a new election starts (after reset), you'll be able to vote again.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

### Button is Disabled

**Possible reasons:**
- Voting hasn't started yet (workflow station 0)
- Voting has ended (workflow station 2)
- You've already voted in this session
- Transaction is processing

### Transaction Failed

**Common causes:**
- Insufficient ETH balance (need 0.01 + gas)
- Wrong network selected
- You've already voted
- Voting phase has ended

**Solution:** Check your wallet balance and network, then try again.

### Vote Not Updating

**If your vote doesn't appear:**
- Wait for transaction confirmation (check your wallet)
- Refresh the page to force data reload
- Verify the transaction on a block explorer
